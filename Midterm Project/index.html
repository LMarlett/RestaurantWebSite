About

Stoicheion Four Serves a four season rotating menu. The ten course menu is based on traditional french dining traditions, updated with techniques utilizing molecular gastronomy. Ingredients are organic and sourced from partnerships with local farms.

The menu is a Prix Fixe selection of the Guest Chef Specialties

The restaurant was founded in 2004 by owners Rachel Franklin and Bob Hogan. The idea for an updated take on the 11 course french meal was created after seeing an old copy of the titanic dining menu. The owners decided to take their expertise in molecular gastronomy and update the old menu. This was also paired trying to source local organic ingredients from farmers and butchers the pair knew personally. All this paired perfectly with wine selections from a local distributor. This takes the notion of handcrafted and hand selected food to a level in which fine dining has not seen yet. All in a rustic Brooklyn setting.
